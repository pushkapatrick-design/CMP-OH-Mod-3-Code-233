Code 233

Standoff Pin Missing or Damaged
Condition
What to Look For
A standoff pin is either completely missing from its mounting position or shows visible signs of damage that compromises its function.
Code Entry
Use Code 233
Record Code 233 when you identify a standoff pin that is missing or damaged during your overhead inspection.
Maintenance Action
No On-Site Work Required
This condition does not require any on-site maintenance. Document the finding and continue your inspection.
